Drawer

Minimal, token-driven drawers with left/right/top/bottom placements and a backdrop. ESC closes, backdrop click closes, and focus returns to the opener.

Tip: the drawer content is just normal HTML. Use buttons, forms, links, etc. The layout is a header/body/footer stack.
