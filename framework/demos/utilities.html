Position helpers

These are fixed-position helpers intended for drawers/menus/toasts. They use --sp-float-gap and --sp-layer-panel.

Try tabbing around to confirm focus rings still behave correctly.

Examples: .sp-pos-top-left .sp-pos-bottom-right .sp-pos-center-right .sp-pos-center

Note: these helpers use position: fixed and pin to the viewport. Apply one .sp-pos-* helper per element.

  • .sp-pos-top-leftviewport top-left
  • .sp-pos-top-rightviewport top-right
  • .sp-pos-bottom-leftviewport bottom-left
  • .sp-pos-bottom-rightviewport bottom-right
  • .sp-pos-center-leftviewport center-left
  • .sp-pos-center-rightviewport center-right
  • .sp-pos-centerviewport center
