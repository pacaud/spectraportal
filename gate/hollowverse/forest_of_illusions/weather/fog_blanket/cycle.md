# fog_blanket — cycle

status: seeded
type: weather_cycle
parent: fog_blanket
region: forest_of_illusions

[typical_duration]
- light fog: 1–3 hours
- blanket fog: 3–8 hours
- afterglow haze: up to the rest of the day in shaded areas

[stages]
- gather
  - air cools; dampness rises; distant edges blur
- settle
  - fog lays down low and steady; visibility shortens evenly
- drift
  - fog shifts in slow sheets, revealing and hiding paths
- lift
  - clears in layers from high to low, leaving a thin hush-haze behind

[conditions]
- more likely: cool mornings, shaded trails, stream bends
- less likely: dry wind, strong sun
