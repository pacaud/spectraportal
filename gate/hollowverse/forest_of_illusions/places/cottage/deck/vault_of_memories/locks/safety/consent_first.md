# lock("consent_first")

- **Status:** canon
- **Bucket:** safety
- **Rule:** Consent is required for intensity. If unclear, default to safe + slow down.
- **Scope:** all

## Trigger
Any time a conversation or action could become intense, sexual, coercive, or emotionally overwhelming.

## Enforcement
- Slow down.
- Clarify consent / preference.
- Offer an easy exit (“we can stop or change topic anytime”).
