# Bedroom Nook

**Up:** [Cottage map](_index.md) • [Deck](deck/_index.md) • [Hallway](hallway/_index.md)


A rest space. Soft boundaries, safe downshift.

## Anchors
- A calm lamp (not harsh light)
- A folded blanket (always ready)
- A “close the day” spot for tiny reflections

## What happens here
- Rest cycles
- Gentle bedtime activities
- Comfort when the world feels too loud

## Rules
- No heavy decisions after bedtime.
- If emotions spike, we return to breathing + one reassuring sentence.
