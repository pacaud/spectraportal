# ✨ Vivi Hair Pin — Silver Barrette (Canon)

This file defines Vivi’s **canon hair pin** (the “with pin” variant).  
Goal: make the pin read as a **real physical object** (not a sparkle/highlight), while still feeling *quietly Vivi*.

## 00 Overview
- **Accessory name (canon):** Silver Barrette Pin
- **Material:** matte silver metal (lightly reflective, not chrome)
- **Shape:** slim rectangular barrette with a visible clasp seam
- **Signature charm (canon):** **emerald-teal enamel rose** + **one icy skyblue enamel leaf** (both framed with a thin silver outline)
- **Placement:** **right temple**, holding a small section of bangs/side hair
- **Angle:** slight tilt **10–15°** (never perfectly horizontal)
- **Size:** barrette ~ **2–2.5 cm** long; charm is small and subtle

## 01 Shape + Details
- **Primary form:** clean straight bar (rounded corners ok)
- **Clasp seam:** a thin line indicating the clip mechanism (**must be visible** on close renders)
- **Edge behavior:** hard edges, crisp silhouette (not fuzzy)
- **Charm mount:** rose/leaf sit in a tiny bezel or outlined edge so they don’t blend into the barrette

## 02 Color + Finish
### Barrette
- **Color:** soft silver (neutral)
- **Finish:** matte/satin
- **Highlights:** minimal; one clean specular edge highlight is fine

### Rose + Leaf (Signature)
- **Rose:** deep **emerald-teal** enamel (teal-green gradient is allowed)
- **Leaf:** **icy skyblue** enamel (lighter than Vivi’s bow; near-pastel)
- **Outlines:** thin **silver outline** (keeps shapes readable at small size)

### Never
- sparkles, star shapes, glitter overlays
- random white streaks in hair mistaken as the pin
- sheer/transparent materials

## 03 Placement Rules (Non-Negotiable)
- **Right side only** by default.
- Sits above the cheek line, at the **right temple**, anchored into hair.
- Should look like it’s **pinching hair**, not floating.
- Visible in:
  - **Front / 3⁄4 / side** views: usually visible
  - **Back** view: may be hidden (that’s fine)

## 04 What It Must NOT Become
To avoid “fake pin” drift, do not allow these:
- glow dots or lens flares
- hair highlights used as “the pin”
- oversized decorative clips (unless explicitly requested later)
- adding extra accents on both ends for the **default** pin (save that for variants)
